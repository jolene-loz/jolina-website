About JOLINA
_

Oh hey there!

I'm Jolina (aka Jolene Lozano) a Filipina-American R&B/Soul-Pop artist from New Jersey. When I was little and being a drama queen, my mom would call me Jolina. But 20+ years later, I realized she was really saying Jolene-ah!!

Needless to say, this marks the start of my new music era: one where I'm unapologetically dramatic, vulnerable, and fully myself. And I make music for those people who are also trying to be in their y2k confident era.
